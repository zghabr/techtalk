1 Frontend Logic

The app toggles the state locally first. This "Optimistic" approach makes the app feel incredibly fast to the user.

2 State Management

The server receives the toggle request and determines if it needs to "Increment" (+1) or "Decrement" (-1) the total.

3 Atomic Updates

The database performs an atomic update to ensure that even if millions of people like at once, the math stays correct.
